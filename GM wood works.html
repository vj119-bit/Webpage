Our Story

Founded on Passion
Built on Precision

G.M. Woodwork Ltd was established in October 1980 by Gilles and Michelle Voyer as an arch specialty product manufacturer and supplier to the building product industry.

What began as a focused craft has grown into a comprehensive architectural millwork company serving builders, contractors, and homeowners with premium wood, clad, and PVC curved-top windows along with decorative components.

We are driven by innovative design and material composition, quality workmanship and total service — making us industry leaders continually striving to exceed our customers' expectations.

Innovation

The Beginning

Founded by Gilles & Michelle Voyer. Focused on decorative products: grilles, louvers, shutters, and wood curve-top window frames.

Clad Expansion

Expanded product line to include clad curve-top frames, bringing weather-resistant cladding technology to the arch window market.

In-House Manufacturing
